# E-Commerce KPI Performance Analysis

## Business Objective
Assess overall business performance using key commercial and customer KPIs, identify growth drivers, and highlight risk areas impacting revenue and conversion.

## Key Business Questions
- How is revenue and order volume trending over time?
- Which product categories and regions contribute most to growth?
- How healthy is the conversion funnel and average order value (AOV)?
- Are there early warning signs in customer retention or repeat purchase?

## Scope of Analysis
- Revenue and growth KPIs
- Customer and retention metrics
- Product and category performance
- Time-series and segment analysis

## Tools
Python, SQL, Pandas, Visualization Libraries
